Travel

From a young age I have always been surrounded by people who loved to travel. I have always been told stories about my uncle who has travelled the globe.

I have heard tales of his travels across Europe, the United states but most stories come from countries in eastern Asia such as China, the Philippines and Thailand.

Not only did I grow up hearing of these tales from my uncle, I also have an older sister who has gone travelling at every opportunity.

She has gone Interrailing across Europe, been to south America and countries in Africa such as Morocco, Nigeria and South Africa. As a result I have the travel bug.
